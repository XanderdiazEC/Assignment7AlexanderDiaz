Contact List Management System

Instructions:

To view the contact list and operations:

  1. Right-click anywhere on this page
  2. Select "Inspect" or press F12
  3. Navigate to the "Console" tab
  4. Use the buttons below to perform operations

Available operations:

  • Displaying the initial contact list
  • Adding a new contact (using the button)
  • Showing the first and last contacts (using the button)
